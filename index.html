About me

My name is Vinicius Ferreira Florencio, I'm 26 years old and I live in São Paulo, Brazil. I'm passionate about pets, video games, fantasy, Nightwish, and technology.

I am a DesignOps & UI/UX Designer with over 2+ years of experience developing large-scale logistics solutions. I have contributed to the ideation and creation of port and airport software used across Brazil and internationally.

Currently, I work at Mosten, where I am responsible for building and maintaining the company's design system and occasionally mentoring new design interns.

I am also completing a degree in Systems Analysis and Development, expected to graduate in December 2025.
