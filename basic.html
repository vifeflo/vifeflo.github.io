This isn't just another UI Kit. It's a flexible multi-brand system built to support multiple brands from a single source of truth. Each brand maintains its unique identity while sharing the same component architecture.

Design Tokens

Token-based system ensuring consistency and scalability. Every design decision is stored as a reusable token, making updates seamless across the entire system.

Colors

Primary, secondary, and semantic colors with light & dark variants. All color combinations are tested for accessibility compliance.

Accessibility & Dark Mode

Built with WCAG guidelines in mind. Every color combination is tested for contrast ratios, and both light and dark modes are designed to be equally accessible.
